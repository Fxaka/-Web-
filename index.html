Explore Technology, Assemble the Future —— Welcome to the Computer Assembly Club!

Members here can actively learn, study, and explore new knowledge, technologies, and applications in the field of science and technology. By personally assembling computers, debugging hardware and software, they hone their hands-on skills and practical abilities, while cultivating a keen insight and creativity for the future development of technology.

Key Features of Our Site:

  • Learn to select computer hardware that meets one's own needs
  • Learn how to assemble a computer
  • Engage in computer hardware exchange activities
  • Participate in computer assembly-related competitions
  • Learn the basics of computer hardware
